* general
** switch buffer (interactively)
   C-x b

* magit
** general
*** magit status
    C-x g
*** expand diff
    tab
*** stage, unstage
    s, u
*** ignore
    i
*** trash (revert change or delete file)
    x

** misc
*** view log for file
    M-x magit-log-buffer-file
*** view file at commit
    M-x magit-find-file
    - run from log to choose revision easily

* dired
** go up a directory
   ^
** open file in other buffer
   shift-RET
** marking
*** mark, unmark file
    m, u
*** invert selection
    t
*** unmark all
    U
*** mark via regex
    %m
*** mark executables, directories, certain extension, etc
    *
** open dired, with current file selected
   M-x dired-jump

* terminal modes
  - M-x term
  - M-x ansi-term
  - M-x eshell

* client/server
** connect to server (do not wait to finish)
   emacsclient -n

** quit client (if called without -n)
   C-x *

* misc
** evaluate lisp statement under cursor
   C-x C-e

* built-in commands
** IRC client
   M-x erc-tls
