* general
** switch buffer (interactively)
   C-x b

* magit
** magit status
   C-x g

** expand diff
   tab

** stage, unstage
   s, u

** ignore
   i

** trash (revert change or delete file)
   x

* client/server
** connect to server (do not wait to finish)
   emacsclient -n

** quit client (if called without -n)
   C-x *

* misc
** evaluate lisp statement under cursor
   C-x C-e
